This wiki is to organize, share, and archive information collected and analyzed by the SPHEREx Calibration Lab. It contains i) detailed instructions for installation of software and drivers for all lab instrumentation; ii) plans and instructions for data collection and storage; iii) schedules; iv) General projects that do not fit into the categories of specific telescope performance or science results should go onto the General Projects logbook.
